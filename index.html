QUANTFLAUNT: Web Design, Development, Branding

QuantFlaunt is a web application that showcases the future of data curation and user narrative. By collecting and sharing not only everyday measureables, but also intangible thoughts and experiences, QuantFlaunt gives a comparable number to users that represents their life. Users of the future aren't satisfied simply measuring their activities—rather than the nuts and bolts, they want the big picture.
