QUANTFLAUNT

QuantFlaunt is an all-in-one library and visualization tool for curated information offline and across the web. The application lets users track literally anything, from the now-outdated everyday of miles ran and calories consumed, to the more progressive datum such as minutiae, moods, and ideologies. The data is collated to give users one total representative number for their identity.

Competitor Review

The iOS "Reporter App" was reviewed during the planning stages of QuantFlaunt. Its data tracking and user created categories centered on activities was a starting point for QuantFlaunt's more progressive tracking.

Read Competitor Review

UX Deliverables

QuantFlaunt went through many iterations, and was rigorously tested with users and classmates. User scenarios and personas gave the application a focus, and rapid wireframes and prototypes were produced in Axure and Bootstrap.

View Deliverables

Final Site

QuantFlaunt went live in 2014. Users can input and track their tangible and intangible thoughts and activities, and compare their representative number to others. An unexpected outcome is the value of a number: is larger better, or is smaller significant of independence?
